ABOUT

The College is one of the most prestigious institutes on the planet. Established in 1972, The College offers an array of courses in multiple disciplines to the students.

A number of nobel laureates, oscar winners, industrial & world leaders were students of the institute. Their great qualities were identified and polished during their studies.

Extra curricular activities and mentorship is provided to each student for a holistic growth of personality.

  • Bachelors and Masters in Arts
  • Bachelors and Masters in Science
  • Bachelors and Masters in Management
  • Bachelors and Masters in Leadership

ACADEMICS

The College provides the best class education to it's students, with the help of top notch faculty from around the world.

Many researchers also give lectures along with experts and leaders of subsequent fields.

A very advanced infrastructure and funding is provided for all the research and project works.

The College also expects a high academic performance from it's students and also rewards the best performers in many fields.

PLACEMENTS

A lot of companies arrive every year at campus for placements and provide students with handsome opportunities and brilliant pay packages.

The College also encourages a lot of entrepreneurial activities and following one's passion rather than a herd chasing a single opportunity.

Many students also prepare for higher studies and further research work, which the institute supports whole heartedly.
